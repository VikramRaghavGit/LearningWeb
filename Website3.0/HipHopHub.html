What is Hip Hop ?

Hip Hop is a style of popular music of US black and Hispanic origin, featuring rap with an electronic backing. Hip-hop combines two slang terms. Hip, which means “in the know,” has been a part of African American vernacular since the late nineteenth century. Hop represents the hopping movement exhibited by hip-hop performers.
